EtherLocked is a first-person puzzle game that I developed together with two classmates over the course of a year as a school project. About the project

EtherLocked is a first-person puzzle game where players solve environmental puzzles using light, perspective, and hidden mechanisms. This project focused heavily on level design, atmosphere, and creating a cohesive player experience.
